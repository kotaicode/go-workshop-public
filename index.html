Golang™ workshop in Frankfurt

Go™ is taking over the software landscape, both in pure coding, and in devops (It is the lingua franca of devops tools). Get hands on training from seasoned Go developers in this workshop.

In a 2-3 day workshop, your coaches will introduce you to Go, work with you through the exercises to enable you to get the hang of the language, and prepare you for writing Go™ in production

What do we focus in our sessions

The workshop will take you through a guided tour of the following – which are special to Golang™

Micro (web) Services

Go™ is slowly becoming the lingua franca of microservice world, we spend quite some time to understand how it fits well

Individual Tools

The tooling of Go™ is as important, as looking at how tools can be built with it. We’ll look at both

Concurrency

One of the most wonderful thing about Go™ is the ease with which it handles concurrency. We will dive into this

Simplicity

What is lovable about Go™ is how it makes everything simple. We’ll have the first glimpse into this

Why Golang™?

  • (Very fast) build
  • Excellent readability
  • Static binary -> easy deployment
  • Lingua franca of web services
  • Easy Async - Goroutines
  • Backed by Google
