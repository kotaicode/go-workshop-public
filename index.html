Golang™ workshop in Frankfurt

Go™ is taking over the software landscape: in development, and in devops (it is the lingua franca of devops tools). Get hands on training from seasoned Go developers in this workshop.

If your company/team is starting with Go, and would like to get a jump start, this workshop is perfect for you. Your coaches will introduce you to Go, work with you through the exercises to enable you to get the hang of the language, and prepare you for writing Go™ in production

What do we focus in our sessions

We jump directly at those which make Golang™ special

Micro (web) Services

Go™ is the lingua franca of microservice world, we will spend quite some time to understand how it fits well

Tooling, and tools

The tooling of Go™ is as important as looking at how tools can be built with it. We’ll look at both

Concurrency

One of the most wonderful things about Go™ is the ease with which it handles concurrency. No workshop is complete without this

Simplicity

Go™ makes your code simple. Nothing but first hand experience will prove this to you

Why Golang™?

  • (Very fast) build
  • Excellent readability
  • Static binary -> easy deployment
  • Lingua franca of web services
  • Easy Async - Goroutines
  • Backed by Google
